Introduction

SALDORET IT & ELECTRONICS COMMUNICATION SERVICES is an Information Technology (ICT) Consultancy Company dedicated to deploying productive, emerging technologies and redeveloping customer's information systems. This proposal outlines an ICT Youth Empowerment Program designed to address the hardships faced by youth in Nigeria, particularly in Yobe State, by providing them with valuable IT skills and entrepreneurial opportunities.

Our services include Corporate Internet connectivity, VSAT, VPN Connectivity, IP-based Intercom solutions, Video Conferencing, CCTV/IP Surveillance, Premises access control, Fire Alarm Systems, Solar Power, Repair and Maintenance, and much more.

Executive Summary

Youth in Nigeria constitute a significant portion of the population, and many face limited opportunities for education, training, employment, health and social services. This often leads to issues like substance abuse and juvenile delinquency, hindering the nation's development. This program aims to nurture and develop underprivileged youths into a productive generation, thereby reducing petty crimes and societal delinquency.

Yobe State, with immense human potential, has youths that must be harnessed. Currently, 54% of these youths are not meaningfully engaged, contributing indirectly to national setbacks. Our program seeks to transform this by equipping them with marketable IT skills.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

At SALDORET, we offer various training services, and we have designed an empowerment scheme covering lucrative information technology skills to foster entrepreneurship among the youth of Yobe State. We are developing a demo website for beneficiary enrollment and proper program documentation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide IT skills and knowledge to Yobe youths, enabling them to recognize and utilize available resources for their holistic empowerment.

Program Details

The program directly targets unemployed and unskilled youths from various local government areas across Yobe State, with a goal to train at least 2000 members.
